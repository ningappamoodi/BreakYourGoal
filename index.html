Privacy Policy for Goal Tracker – Break Your Goal

Last updated: February 2025

Goal Tracker – Break Your Goal (“the App”) is developed by ningappamoodi. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles user data.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect any personal or sensitive user data, including email, name, phone number, location, or device identifiers.

User Goal Data (Local Only)

The only information entered by the user is the goal/task added inside the app. This data is:

  • Stored locally on the user’s device
  • Not uploaded to any server
  • Not shared with anyone

2. Data Storage

All data is saved only on the device in local storage. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes this data.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services such as:

  • AdMob / Ads
  • Analytics (Firebase, Google Analytics, etc.)
  • Crash reporting tools
  • External servers

4. Permissions

The App does not request or use any sensitive permissions.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 13 and above. No personal data is collected from children under 13.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with advertisers, third parties, or any external services.

7. Security

Your data (goals/tasks) remains private on your device. No information is transmitted outside.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy if the app features change. The updated policy will always be posted on this page.
